# Financeo - Personal Finance & Budgeting
Financeo is a secure, private, and easy-to-use personal finance and budgeting website built with Python and Django. It's designed to help you take control of your financial life by tracking income, managing expenses, and sticking to your budgets.

## Features

- Secure User Authentication: Standard secure registration, login, logout, and password reset functionality. Your financial data is for your eyes only.
- Account Management: Track multiple financial accounts (checking, savings, credit cards, etc.).
- Budget Management: Set monthly (or other periodic) budgets by category and monitor your progress.
- Simple and effective reports (pie charts, bar charts) to visualize your spending habits and income vs. expenses.

## Tech Stack

**Client:** Django

**Server:** Mysql
